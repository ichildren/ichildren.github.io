    # 暴龙（T-Rex）

    # 中文名：暴龙

    # 英文名：Tyrannosaurus Rex

    # 简介：

    暴龙是最著名的肉食性恐龙之一，也被称为 "霸王龙"。它是最大的肉食性恐龙之一，拥有强大的颚部肌肉和巨大的牙齿。 Tyrannosaurus Rex, also known as "T-Rex," is one of the most famous carnivorous dinosaurs. It is one of the largest meat-eating dinosaurs, equipped with powerful jaw muscles and enormous teeth.

    暴龙生活在白垩纪晚期，是顶级掠食者，被认为是当时恐龙王国的霸主。 T-Rex lived during the late Cretaceous Period and was considered a top predator, often regarded as the ruler of the dinosaur kingdom at that time.
