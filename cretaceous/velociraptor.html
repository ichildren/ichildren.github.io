    # 迅猛龙（Velociraptor）

    # 中文名：迅猛龙

    # 英文名：Velociraptor

    # 简介：

    迅猛龙是白垩纪时期的一种小型肉食性恐龙，以其敏捷性和智慧而著名。 Velociraptor is a small carnivorous dinosaur from the Cretaceous period, known for its agility and intelligence.

    它通常生活在群体中，通过协同狩猎来捕捉猎物。 It typically lived in groups and cooperated in hunting to capture prey.

    迅猛龙拥有锋利的爪子，特别是第二趾上的巨大而锯齿状的爪子，用来抓住和剖开猎物。 Velociraptor had sharp claws, particularly the huge and serrated ones on its second toes, which it used to grasp and dismember prey.

    与电影中的形象不同，迅猛龙通常比人类高度低，但它在恐龙界仍然是一种聪明且具有攻击性的掠食者。 Contrary to its portrayal in movies, Velociraptor was usually smaller than humans in height, but it remained an intelligent and predatory dinosaur within the world of dinosaurs.

    它的化石发现表明迅猛龙可能拥有羽毛，这使得它成为恐龙演化中的有趣物种。 Fossil evidence suggests that Velociraptor may have had feathers, making it an intriguing species in the evolution of dinosaurs.

    迅猛龙是白垩纪时期恐龙多样性的一部分，展示了恐龙王国中不同层面的生态角色。 Velociraptor was a part of the diversity of dinosaurs in the Cretaceous period, showcasing different ecological roles within the dinosaur kingdom.
