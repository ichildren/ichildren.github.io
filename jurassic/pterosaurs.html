    # 翼龙（Pterosaurs）

    # 中文名：翼龙

    # 英文名：Pterosaurs

    # 简介：

    翼龙其实并不是恐龙，但它们生活在和恐龙同一时代。 Pterosaurs were not actually dinosaurs, but they lived in the same time as the dinosaurs.

    很久很久以前。它们特殊的地方在于它们可以飞翔，就像鸟类或飞机一样！ A very long time ago. They were special because they could fly, just like birds or airplanes!

    翼龙有大大的翅膀，可以让它们在天空中滑翔。 Pterosaurs had big wings that allowed them to glide through the sky.

    有些翼龙很小，就像鸟一样，而有些竟然有成年人那么大！ Some of them were small, like birds, and others were as big as an adult human!

    这些飞行生物有锋利的喙，喜欢捉鱼吃，就像今天的鸟类一样。 These flying creatures had sharp beaks and liked to catch fish from the water, just like birds do today.

    有些甚至有特别的头冠，让它们看起来非常独特和迷人。 Some even had special crests on their heads, making them look unique and fascinating.

    翼龙有各种各样的形状和大小，它们能够在古老的天空中翱翔。 Pterosaurs came in many different shapes and sizes, and they could soar through the ancient skies.

    虽然它们现在已经不存在了，但我们仍然可以通过它们的化石来了解它们。 Even though they are not around anymore, we can still learn about them from their fossils.
